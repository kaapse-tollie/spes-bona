# Spes Bona - Culture Overrides
# REPLACE: directive overwrites the vanilla griqua culture definition entirely.
#
# BUG FIX: Vanilla V3 incorrectly assigns griqua heritage as heritage_bantu.
# The Griqua were a mixed-race community of primarily Khoisan and European (Dutch) descent,
# not Bantu. They descended from Cape Khoikhoi, escaped slaves, and Dutch settlers/soldiers.
# heritage_khoisan is the correct classification.
#
# The Griqua spoke Dutch/Afrikaans (language_dutch) and were predominantly Protestant
# (converted by missionaries, particularly the London Missionary Society).
# religion = protestant and language = dutch are both historically accurate.
#
# graphics = european and ethnicities weighted toward african_diaspora (2:1 vs african)
# reflect their mixed heritage and European-influenced material culture (clothing, firearms, wagons).
#
# Cross-ref: common/country_definitions/sb_countries.txt — ORL (Oorlam) uses griqua as primary culture.
